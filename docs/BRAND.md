# Bin Askar Technology — Brand direction

## Concept

The identity is **Technical Authority with a Human Signature**. The monogram joins a strong B with an upward A: a visual bridge between Abdullah’s technical leadership and the company’s delivery work.

## Logo system

- Primary mark: the geometric BA monogram in `src/components/brand-mark.tsx`.
- Dark lockup: ink-green field with brass B and paper-white A.
- Small-size use: use the mark alone; keep clear space equal to one quarter of the mark width.
- Never stretch, rotate, outline, or place the mark over a busy image.
- The favicon and Apple touch icon use the same mark so the identity stays consistent outside the page.

## Color

| Token | Hex | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ink | `#14231E` | Navigation, hero, footer, primary text |
| Brass | `#B8792B` | Mark, actions, rules, emphasis |
| Paper | `#F3EFE7` | Page background |
| Canvas | `#FFFDF8` | Cards and form surfaces |
| Moss | `#53635C` | Supporting text |

## Voice and composition

Use confident, concise, editorial layouts. Lead with the founder’s judgment, show the work through case studies, then make contact easy. The visual language should feel measured and premium—not playful, glossy, or startup-generic.

## Messaging direction

- Lead with the client decision and its business consequence, not a list of technologies.
- Position the company as a founder-led technology advisory practice that stays close to delivery.
- Describe every service through the situation it resolves and the output the client can own.
- Prefer concrete language: direction, architecture, delivery, governance, evaluation, and knowledge transfer.
- Avoid generic “digital journey” language, inflated transformation claims, and unsupported performance metrics.
